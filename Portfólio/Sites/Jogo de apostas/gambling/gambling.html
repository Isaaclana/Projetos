Hello , this is how it works:

This is a Dice Gambling. There will be two "dice" on the screen and you need to guess how much both of them will get. As you know, a normal dice only have six sides, so with two dice it'll be twelve, with that in mind you can bet a number between 2 and 12. You start with 50 points from the beginning and when you guess the right number that amount that you bet will be tripled.
